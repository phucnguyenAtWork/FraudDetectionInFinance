Why Tuning Matters

Default models already catch 89–92% of fraud. Tuning is about operational efficiency: XGBoost tuning trades 8 missed fraud cases for 279 fewer false alarms (43% reduction). At scale, this means fewer customer disruptions and lower investigation costs.

Conclusion

Key Takeaways

1

SMOTE makes the invisible visible

Without it, models default to "predict no fraud" and catch nothing. SMOTE increased fraud representation by 17.2x, enabling 89–92% recall.
2

Tuning optimizes the trade-off

Default models already catch most fraud. Tuning reduces false alarms by up to 43% (279 fewer per cycle), meaning fewer customer disruptions.
3

The pipeline scales

Spark MLlib achieves 98.97% AUC on the same data, proving the approach works on distributed infrastructure for millions of daily transactions.
